Every dish on this board is 100% vegetarian comfort food — the way it's meant to be eaten: tear the warm bread with your hand, scoop up the curry, and take the perfect bite.

Strictly Vegetarian, Strictly Delicious
01 — THE TANDOOR & THE GRIDDLE

The Breads

From clay-oven puffed naan to hand-stretched rumali roti — every bread here exists to be torn, not cut.

Butter Naan

Refined wheat flour, yeast & yogurt, clay-oven baked, finished with a pool of melted butter.

Laccha Paratha

Whole wheat flour layered with clarified butter, pan-fried into a crispy, multi-layered flatbread.

Bhatura

Refined wheat flour, yogurt & semolina, deep-fried into a large, hollow puffed bread.

Garlic Naan

Refined wheat naan topped with minced raw garlic and fresh cilantro, clay-oven baked.

Stuffed Paratha

Whole wheat flatbread stuffed with spiced mashed potato or fresh cottage cheese, pan-fried in ghee.

Butter Roti

Thin whole wheat flatbread cooked on a flat griddle, brushed with melted butter.

Rumali Roti

Paper-thin, extremely soft flatbread, hand-stretched and cooked over a hot inverted dome pan.

Amritsari Kulcha

Dough stuffed with spiced mashed potatoes, onions & coriander seeds, baked until crispy.

02 — THE GRAVY BOWLS

The Curries

Slow-simmered, butter-finished, and built for scooping — these are the gravies every bread on this page was born to meet.

Paneer Butter Masala

Fresh cottage cheese cubes in a smooth sauce of puréed tomatoes, cashew paste, cream & butter.

Dal Makhani

Whole black lentils & kidney beans slow-cooked for hours with tomato purée, cream & butter.

Chole

Chickpeas simmered in a dark gravy of onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic & roasted spices.

Kadai Paneer

Cottage cheese, chunky bell peppers & onions in a coarsely pounded coriander-chili spice blend.

Veg Korma

Mixed vegetables in a mildly spiced gravy of yogurt, cashew paste & whole spices.

Malai Kofta

Fried potato-paneer dumplings stuffed with nuts, bathed in a creamy cashew-onion sauce.

Paneer Tikka Masala

Charred, yogurt-marinated cottage cheese cubes tossed in a thick onion-tomato gravy.

Pindi Chole

Chickpeas boiled with tea leaves for color, cooked dry with roasted cumin & pomegranate seeds.

04 — NO BREAD REQUIRED

The Absolute Standalone Classics

These dishes need no bread partner — they arrive whole, complete, and completely their own thing.

Rajma Chawal

Red kidney beans slow-cooked in a savory onion-tomato gravy, served over steaming basmati rice.

Non-negotiable duo

Vada Pav

A deep-fried, spiced mashed potato ball in chickpea batter, sandwiched in a bread roll with garlic & green chili chutneys.

Golgappe (Pani Puri)

Ultra-crisp fried shells stuffed with spiced potato & chickpeas, filled with chilled tangy mint water and tamarind chutney.

Masala Dosa

Giant, paper-thin crepe from fermented rice & lentil batter, wrapped around turmeric-spiced potato filling. Served with sambar & coconut chutney.

Samosa Chaat

A flaky fried pastry stuffed with spiced potatoes and peas, smashed open under warm chickpea curry, yogurt & sweet-spicy chutneys.

Pav Bhaji

A thick, spicy vegetable mash cooked on a buttery griddle, served with soft bread rolls toasted in butter.
